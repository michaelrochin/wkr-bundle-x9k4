STRAIGHT ANSWERS

Questions people actually ask.

How is this $97 and not $99/month like VideoAsk?

The $97 (or $297 for the Agency plan) is a one-time payment for the Stokereel tool itself — that's the whole transaction with us.

After you check out, you get step-by-step setup instructions for plugging Stokereel into your own Cloudflare account, which is where your videos actually live. We are not Cloudflare and we have no affiliation with them — you create your own free account directly, you own your data, and you can pull it out anytime.

Cloudflare is the same infrastructure company that Discord, Shopify, IBM, DoorDash, OpenAI, Stripe, Loom, and Zendesk run on. So your testimonials sit on the same network those companies use — not some no-name host.

Cloudflare's pricing is brutally cheap because they don't charge for downloads (unlike AWS). Storage is $0.015 per GB per month. A typical 60-second testimonial video is ~10 MB. So in real numbers:

  • 100 testimonials ≈ 1 GB ≈ $0.015/mo (basically free)
  • 1,000 testimonials ≈ 10 GB ≈ $0.15/mo
  • 10,000 testimonials ≈ 100 GB ≈ $1.50/mo

Compare to VideoAsk's $99/mo from day one — that's $1,188/year regardless of whether you collect 5 testimonials or 5,000. Over 5 years: $5,940 with VideoAsk vs. maybe $10–$50 total with Stokereel.
