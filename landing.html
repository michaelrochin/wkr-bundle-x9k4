How it works (60 seconds, three steps)

  1. Click "Deploy to Cloudflare" above

    Sign in to (or create) a free Cloudflare account. StokeReel auto-deploys into your account and creates an R2 bucket for you.

FAQs

Do I really pay nothing per month?
The first ~10GB of storage (50–100 testimonials) is free on Cloudflare R2 forever. After that it's $0.015/GB/month — about $1.50/month at 100GB. Bandwidth (egress) is unmetered.
Where do my videos live?
In your own Cloudflare R2 bucket. Not mine. You own them, you can download them, you can delete them.
What if Cloudflare goes down?
Cloudflare's edge network has 99.99% uptime — better than VideoAsk by design. If it ever fully goes down, the rest of the internet does too.
Does this work on GoHighLevel? Webflow? WordPress?
Yes. The recorder works as a hosted URL (just send the link) or as a one-line iframe embed in any HTML/Custom Code block — GHL, WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, ClickFunnels, Kajabi, Carrd, anywhere.
What about HIPAA / GDPR?
Your data lives in your account. You sign your DPA directly with Cloudflare. No third party (not even me) is a data processor.
Can I customize without code?
Yes. The branding dashboard at /config lets you change colors, fonts, copy, questions, button labels — and shows live previews of every change.
What if I need help?
Most setups take under 10 minutes self-serve. If you'd rather not handle it yourself, see the done-for-you option below.
